Why NEXT Field Connect?

  • Farmers struggle with limited access to reliable agricultural data.
  • Unstable market prices make it hard to sell at the right time.
  • Changing climate conditions demand better planning.
  • High-quality seeds and expert guidance are not always accessible.

NEXT Field Connect solves all these problems by delivering instant, AI-powered agricultural insights at your fingertips.

How NEXT Field Connect Works

  1. Send a question on WhatsApp, e.g., "What is the price of maize today?"
  2. Our AI system retrieves real-time data from a trusted agricultural database.
  3. You get an instant response, e.g., "Maize is selling at 400 Rwf/kg in Musanze District."
  4. Continue asking about seeds, climate, or expert advice.

No complex apps—just WhatsApp!

Key Features
